Our History

Founded in 1967, White Water Rafting has been dedicated to providing unforgettable river adventures for over many years. Starting as a small local outfitter, we've grown into a premier rafting company, passionate about sharing the beauty and thrill of the rivers. Our commitment to safety, environmental stewardship, and customer satisfaction has been the cornerstone of our journey. We believe in creating experiences that last a lifetime, fostering a deep appreciation for nature and the excitement of the rapids.

Over the decades, we've expanded our routes and modernized our equipment, but our core values remain the same: adventure, safety, and respect for the environment. We've guided thousands of adventurers, from first-timers to seasoned pros, through some of the most breathtaking landscapes. Join us and become part of our ongoing story on the river now a days!
